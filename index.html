XMPP plugins for Strophe v0.2

A collection of Strophe plugins covering the essential parts of

  • Instant Messaging
  • XEP-0060 Publish-Subscribe
  • XEP-0004: Data Forms
  • XEP-0054: vcard-temp
  • XEP-0049: Private XML storage
  • Roster management & Roster Item Exchange

Documentation

Please refer to the annotated source:

  • strophe.messaging.js
  • strophe.pubsub.js
  • strophe.forms.js
  • strophe.vcard.js
  • strophe.private.js
  • strophe.roster.js

Dependencies

strophe.plugins is designed for use in the context of Backbone and therefore depends (additionally to Strophe) on Backbone and Underscore.
